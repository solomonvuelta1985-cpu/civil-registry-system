# CRDMS — Civil Registry Digital Management System
## System Showcase Summary

> *"Behind every certificate is a person, and behind every record is a responsibility. The records we safeguard today become the heritage of tomorrow, and how we preserve them reflects how much we value the people they belong to."*

---

The CRDMS is a comprehensive digital records management solution developed exclusively for the Municipal Civil Registrar's Office of the Municipality of Baggao, headed by Sir Atanacio G. Tungpalan. What makes this system remarkable is not just what it can do, but where it runs and what that means for the municipality's most sensitive public records. The entire platform is deployed on a Synology DS925+ NAS server housed within the office itself, ensuring that every birth certificate, marriage record, death certificate, and license application remains physically stored on the municipality's own hardware and never passes through external servers or third-party cloud services. This deliberate choice of on-premises NAS infrastructure gives the Municipality of Baggao complete data sovereignty over its civil registry, while still allowing authorized staff to access the system securely from any location through a Cloudflare Tunnel.

The Synology NAS provides reliable, always-on storage with the capacity to scale alongside the registry's growing archive for years to come, and the system's zero external software dependencies and full offline capability guarantee that civil registry operations continue uninterrupted even during internet outages. Built through 45 iterations of careful development and refinement, CRDMS was purpose-built to solve the challenges that have long burdened civil registry work: the slow and error-prone nature of paper-based record keeping, the difficulty of retrieving certificates buried in years of physical filing, and the constant risk of document loss or deterioration over time. By bringing together intelligent document scanning, automated data extraction, and a structured digital workflow into a single unified platform, CRDMS empowers the Civil Registrar staff and encoders of the Municipality of Baggao to digitize, manage, and safeguard all four types of civil registry documents, namely Certificates of Live Birth, Certificates of Marriage, Certificates of Death, and Applications for Marriage License.

What sets CRDMS apart from ordinary document management systems is the depth of capability built into every feature. The system's intelligent OCR (Optical Character Recognition), powered by Tesseract, can read a scanned PDF certificate and automatically extract names, dates, places, and other critical fields into the data entry form, complete with confidence scoring that tells the encoder exactly how reliable each extracted value is. This alone transforms what used to be hours of manual typing into a process that takes minutes. Staff can scan physical documents directly from the browser using the integrated Epson DS-530 II hardware scanner without ever opening separate scanning software, and then immediately verify accuracy through a side-by-side PDF Comparison Viewer that places the original scanned image next to the encoded data. Every certificate passes through a six-state workflow engine, moving from Draft to Pending Review, Verified, Approved, and finally Archived, with each transition recorded alongside the responsible user and timestamp so there is always a clear chain of accountability. For large-scale historical digitization, a batch upload system with auto-OCR and auto-validation allows the office to process years of backlogged paper records efficiently. And when any record needs to be found, the advanced search engine uses a smart two-pass matching algorithm that delivers results in seconds across the entire registry, whether searching by name, registry number, date, or location.

It is through Sir Atanacio G. Tungpalan's forward-thinking initiative and willingness to embrace digitalization that a system like CRDMS became possible within the Municipal Civil Registrar's Office of Baggao. At a time when many local government offices still rely entirely on manual processes, Sir Tungpalan recognized the value of transitioning to a digital platform that could protect, organize, and future-proof the municipality's civil registry records. That vision is now realized in a platform that gives encoders dedicated forms tailored to each certificate type, PDF attachment with SHA-256 integrity hashing that instantly detects if any document has been tampered with or corrupted, automatic backup versioning so no file is ever permanently lost, and a three-state record lifecycle (Active, Archived, Deleted) with soft-delete protection that prevents accidental permanent deletion. For administrators, the system provides a full user management interface with role-based access control across three tiers (Admin, Encoder, Viewer) and permissions configurable down to the individual certificate type, a real-time security monitoring dashboard that flags suspicious activity and failed login attempts, a PDF integrity scanner that can audit every document in the archive and restore any damaged file from a verified backup with a single click, and device lock security that restricts system access to registered devices only. Multiple layers of protection, from CSRF tokens and rate-limited login attempts to session hardening and complete activity logging of every action performed in the system, ensure that the sensitive personal information entrusted to the Civil Registrar's Office remains secure at all times. With purpose-built software running on dedicated Synology NAS hardware right inside the office, the CRDMS equips the Municipality of Baggao's Civil Registrar's Office with the tools it needs to deliver faster, more accurate, and more reliable civil registry services to every resident who walks through its doors.
